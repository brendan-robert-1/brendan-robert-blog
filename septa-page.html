SEPTA: Mobile Ticketing

SEPTA (South Eastern Pennsylvania Transit Authority) is my local transit authority. I helped develop and deploy a "Mobile Ticketing" initiative. Previously the only way to pay a fare is with cash or a SEPTA "KeyCard" there were no fully virtual/mobile options. I was the lead back-end software engineer responsible for connecting SEPTA's existing functionality into a third pary mobile ticketing API. Security and performance were huge concerns.

Additionally I worked on encoding and decoding QR code functionality. This would allow bus and rail workers to use equipment to scan QR codes on users phones. This information was decoded and routed through SEPTA's existing functionality as well as incorporating calls to the third party mobile ticketing api.

Key languages and tools: AWS (Lambda, SNS, EC2, S3), PHP, Linux, REST
